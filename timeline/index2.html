Timeline and Juxtapose Assignments

Welcome to the Wonderful World of Harry Potter

British author J.K. Rowling kicked off the world wide phenomenon that is the Harry Potter franchise in 1997 with the publication of "Harry Potter and the Philospher's Stone." The novel introduced the world to Harry Potter on his eleventh birthday as he learns that he is a wizard and all of the excitement, drama and danger that ensues as he dives deeper into the world. The multi-billion-dollar film franchise was kicked off in 2001 with the release of the first film starring Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson and Rupert Grint. The franchise includes eight films and brought in over $7 billion dollars throughout its ten year span.

Queen Elizabeth Throughout Time

Queen Elizabeth was the Queen of the United Kingdom and other Commonwealths from Feb. 1952 until her death in Sept. 2022. She reigned for 70 years and 214 days, making her the longest reigning British monarch. She also holds the title for the longest verified reign of any female monarch in history.
